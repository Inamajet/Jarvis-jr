# JARVIS Jr.

An ESP32-CAM powered desk companion robot.

## What it does

JARVIS Jr. is a desk companion robot that watches my room with its camera, listens for a double-clap to wake up, and responds with a servo arm that waves, points, or holds things. plus an LED face that shows expressions. Think of it as a smart speaker that actually does things instead of just talking. it can also look at my monitor using its camera to help me with penetration testing and other stuff.


## Why I'm building it

Alexa can't wave back. Every smart assistant on the market just sits there and talks. I wanted something with a physical presence that actually interacts with the room. And as the name suggests i want it to be like jarvis from ironman.
